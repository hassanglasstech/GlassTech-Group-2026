## Slide 2: Solution

**AI Agent Operating System for Manufacturing**

Staff sends: "tanker aaya hai 2000 ka"

Owner approves with one tap.

- **EventOS:** Natural language → ERP workflow (no forms, no training)
- **Decision Intelligence:** Agents learn from owner feedback (gets smarter)
- **IFRS Compliant:** IAS 2, IFRS 15, IAS 24 built-in
- **PKR 75K/month** (100x cheaper than SAP)
