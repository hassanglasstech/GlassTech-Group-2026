## Company Name
GlassTech AgentOS (working name — may rebrand to "ManufactureAI" or "FactoryOS")

## One-Liner
AI Agent Operating System for Pakistani manufacturing SMEs — staff send WhatsApp messages in Urdu, agents execute ERP workflows automatically.

## What are you building? (200 words)

We built an AI-powered ERP where factory staff send messages like "tanker aaya hai 2000 ka" (water tanker arrived, PKR 2000) and AI agents classify the event, assemble a workflow (create requisition, record payment, post GL entry), and execute after owner approval. No forms. No training. No IT department needed.

The system has three layers: EventOS (converts unstructured messages to structured workflows), Decision Intelligence (learns from owner feedback — gets smarter over time), and IFRS-compliant financial automation (IAS 2 inventory, IFRS 15 revenue recognition, IAS 24 intercompany).

We built this for our own 5-company glass manufacturing group (370+ production files, 96 Supabase tables, 25 agent tools). Now productizing as SaaS for Pakistan's 30,000+ manufacturers stuck on Excel and WhatsApp.
