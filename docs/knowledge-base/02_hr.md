## Core Workflows (Step-by-Step)

### Workflow 1: Employee Onboarding (Hiring)

**Screen:** Human Capital (HCM) > Employee Management

1. Click **+ Add Employee**
2. Fill in Personal Information:
   - Name (required)
   - CNIC (optional, format: 35201-1234567-1 -- HR-4 validation)
   - Phone (optional, format: 03XX-XXXXXXX -- HR-5 validation)
   - Address, Emergency Contact
3. Fill in Work Information:
   - Department (select from department list)
   - Designation (assign tag from Tag Master)
   - Grade, Join Date
   - Employee Code auto-generated: `{COMPANY}-{SEQUENCE}` (e.g., GTK-001)
   - Status defaults to **Probation**
4. Fill in Salary Structure:
   - Basic Pay
   - House Rent Allowance
   - Conveyance Allowance
   - Special Allowance
   - Medical Allowance (optional)
   - Fuel Allowance (optional)
   - EOBI toggle (PKR 370/month deduction if enabled)
5. Upload Required Documents:
   - Photo (compressed to 400px, JPEG 0.7)
   - CNIC Front & Back (with expiry date)
   - Police Verification (with expiry date)
   - Job Letter
   - Contract (optional, with expiry date)
6. Click **Save** -- employee created, stored in Supabase

### Workflow 2: Daily Attendance Entry

**Screen:** Human Capital (HCM) > Attendance Register

**Daily Mode:**
1. Select date from calendar
2. For each employee, mark status:
   - **Present** -- normal working day
   - **Absent** -- absent without leave
   - **Late** -- enter late minutes (will count toward penalty)
3. Enter overtime hours if applicable
4. Click **Save Day**

**Monthly Bulk Mode:**
1. Select month (YYYY-MM)
2. Grid view shows all employees x all days
3. Click cells to toggle status
4. Click **Save All**

**Summary Override Mode:**
1. Select employee for the month
2. Manually set: Absent count, Allowed absent, Late count, Sunday premium, OT hours
3. Set Manual Loan Deduction (-1 = use default, 0 = waive loan -- requires manager+ role)
4. Click **Save Override** -- stored in `attendance_overrides` table

**Key Rule -- Sandwich Sundays:** If Saturday OR Monday is absent, the Sunday between them is auto-counted as absent (no explicit entry needed).

### Workflow 3: Leave Application & Approval

**Screen:** Human Capital (HCM) > Leave Management

**Employee Applies:**
1. Click **Apply for Leave**
2. Select Leave Type: Annual (16 days), Casual (10), Sick (8), Unpaid (unlimited), Maternity (90), Paternity (3)
3. Select From Date and To Date
4. Enter Reason
5. Click **Submit** -- status becomes `Pending`

**Manager Approves:**
1. Open Leave Management, filter **Pending** applications
2. Review the request details
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject** (with optional note)
4. On Approval: System auto-creates attendance records as `Leave` for each weekday in the range (Sundays excluded)
5. Leave balance updated: used[type] += approved days

**Leave Balance Calculation:**
- Fiscal year: January 1 -- December 31
- Remaining = Entitlement - Used this year
- Carryforward: Up to 6 days of unused Annual leave from prior year
- Day count excludes Sundays (working days only: Monday-Saturday)

### Workflow 4: Payroll Generation

**Screen:** Human Capital (HCM) > Payroll Management

1. Select **Month** (YYYY-MM format)
2. Click **Generate Payroll**
3. System auto-calculates for each active employee:

**Calculation Steps:**
```
a) Salary Days = Max(20, 25 - public holidays on working days)
b) Day Rate = Gross Salary / Salary Days
c) Hourly Rate = Day Rate / 8

d) Absent Deduction = Final Absent Count x Day Rate
   (includes sandwich Sundays, minus allowed absences from override)

e) Late Penalty = Floor(Late Count / 3) x Day Rate
   (every 3 lates = 1 penalty day)

f) Overtime Pay = OT Hours x Hourly Rate x 1.5

g) EOBI Deduction = PKR 370 (if employee.salary.eobi = true)

h) Loan Deduction = Sum of monthly repayments for Active loans
i) Advance Deduction = Sum of one-time advance amounts

j) Total Deductions capped at 50% of (Gross - Absent - Late - EOBI)
   (Loans applied first, advances get remainder of cap)

k) Net Salary = Gross + Overtime - Absent - Late - EOBI - Loans - Advances
   (Floor at 0 -- cannot go negative)
```

4. Review payroll summary table (employee, gross, deductions, net)
5. Click **Approve Payroll** -- locks the month

**Salary Disbursement (GL Posting):**
1. Mark employee as **Salary Paid**
2. GL Entry: Dr Salaries Payable / Cr Cash in Hand
3. Mark **OT Paid** separately: Dr Salaries Payable / Cr Cash in Hand

### Workflow 5: Loan & Advance Management

**Screen:** Human Capital (HCM) > Loan Management

**Issue Loan:**
1. Click **+ New Loan/Advance**
2. Select Employee, enter Amount, Date
3. Select Type: **Loan** (monthly installments) or **Advance** (one-time deduction)
4. For Loans: enter monthly repayment amount
5. Optionally link to approved Requisition (auto-fills amount)
6. Click **Save** -- status becomes `Active`
7. GL Entry: Dr Staff Loans & Advances (1121) / Cr Cash in Hand (1111)

**Deduction in Payroll:**
- Active loans: repaymentAmount deducted monthly
- Active advances: full amount deducted in next salary
- Skip logic: Set `skipMonth = 'YYYY-MM'` to skip one month
- Waiver: Set `manualLoanDeduction = 0` in override (requires manager+ role -- HR-3)

**Loan Paid Off:**
- When fully repaid, status changes to `Paid`
- No further deductions

### Workflow 6: Employee Transfer (Inter-Company)

1. Open Employee record
2. Click **Transfer**
3. Select destination company
4. Enter reason and approver name
5. Click **Confirm Transfer**
6. Transfer history entry created: fromCompany, toCompany, date, reason, approvedBy
7. Employee.company updated to new company
8. Payroll switches to new company from next month

### Workflow 7: Disciplinary Actions

**Screen:** Human Capital (HCM) > Disciplinary Manager

1. Click **+ New Action**
2. Select Employee, Date, Type:
   - Verbal Warning
   - Written Warning
   - Show Cause Notice
   - Suspension
   - Termination
3. Enter Subject and Details
4. Issued By defaults to HR
5. Click **Save**
6. Employee must acknowledge (marks acknowledged = true with date)

**Escalation Path:** Verbal Warning > Written Warning > Show Cause > Suspension > Termination

## Strict Business Rules & Constraints

### HR-1: Identity Verification in Approvals
- Approver identity resolved from JWT-verified profile (server-side)
- No free-text approver name input (prevents spoofing)

### HR-2: Public Holiday Handling
- Holidays fetched from `public_holidays` table per company
- Only holidays on working days (Mon-Sat) reduce salary days
- Sundays excluded (already off)
- Minimum salary days: 20 (prevents corrupt data)
- Offline fallback: 0 holidays (conservative, does not penalize employees)

### HR-3: Loan Waiver Authorization
- Setting `manualLoanDeduction = 0` in override = loan waiver request
- **Only authorized roles:** `manager`, `hr_manager`, `finance_manager`, `super_admin`
- Unauthorized users: override silently ignored, default deduction applied
- Prevents unauthorized waivers even if DB record is tampered

### HR-4: CNIC Format Validation
- Pattern: `^\d{5}-\d{7}-\d{1}$` (e.g., 35201-1234567-1)
- Optional field -- blank allowed during onboarding
- Must match NADRA standard if provided

### HR-5: Pakistani Mobile Validation
- Pattern: `^(03\d{2})-?\d{7}$` (e.g., 0300-1234567)
- Optional field
- Must match PTA standard if provided

### SEC-2: Multi-Tenant Isolation
- All HR queries scoped to company field
- Application-layer guard + Supabase RLS (defense-in-depth)
- No cross-tenant data leakage possible

### Payroll Loan Cap
- Combined loan + advance deductions cannot exceed 50% of net salary (after absent/late/EOBI deductions)
- Loans applied first, advances get remainder

### Late Penalty Formula
- Every 3 lates = 1 penalty day (floor-based: `Math.floor(lateCount / 3)`)
- Penalty deducted at daily rate

### Overtime Rate
- 1.5x hourly rate (time-and-a-half)
- Hourly rate = Day Rate / 8 hours

### EOBI Deduction
- Fixed PKR 370/month if employee.salary.eobi is enabled
- No EOBI if not registered

## State Machines

### Employee Status
```
Probation ----[Confirm]----> Confirmed
    |                            |
    +----[Resign]--------------> Resigned
    +----[Terminate]-----------> Terminated
    +----[Suspend]-------------> Suspended
```
**Inactive statuses** (excluded from active lists): `resigned`, `terminated`

### Leave Application Status
```
Pending ----[Manager Approves]----> Approved (auto-marks attendance)
   |
   +----[Manager Rejects]--------> Rejected
   |
   +----[Employee Cancels]-------> Cancelled
```

### Loan/Advance Status
```
Active ----[Fully Repaid]----> Paid
  |
  +----[Skip Month]---------> Active (skips one month deduction)
```

### Attendance Status Values
- **Present** -- normal working day
- **Absent** -- absent without leave
- **Late** -- present but arrived late (saved as Present in DB with late_minutes > 0)
- **Leave** -- on approved leave (saved as Absent in DB)

## GL Impact

### Salary Disbursement
- **Dr:** Salaries Payable (2211 -- Current Liability)
- **Cr:** Cash in Hand (1111 -- Current Asset)
- Amount: Basic + Allowances - Absent - Late - Loans - Advances

- **Dr:** Salaries Payable (2211)
- **Cr:** Cash in Hand (1111)
- Amount: OT hours x hourly rate x 1.5

### Loan/Advance Issuance
- **Dr:** Staff Loans & Advances (1121 -- Current Asset)
- **Cr:** Cash in Hand (1111)
- Amount: Loan/Advance amount issued

## Compensation Analysis

### Retention Risk Scoring
- Market gap > 30%: +3 points
- Market gap > 15%: +2 points
- Financial stress (2+ loans or loan > 2x salary): +2 points
- Tenure > 3 years AND gap > 20%: +2 points
- **Risk Level:** Critical (6+), High (4+), Medium (2+), Low (<2)

### Recommended Raise Formula
```
candidateRaise = max(marketGap x 0.5, salary x 0.08)
cappedRaise = min(candidateRaise, salary x 0.25)
roundedRaise = round to nearest PKR 500
```

### Replacement Cost
- Top performers: 6x monthly salary
- Others: 4x monthly salary

### Performance Tiers (from KPI)
- Top: efficiency >= 90%
- Good: efficiency >= 75%
- Average: efficiency >= 60%
- Below: efficiency < 60%
